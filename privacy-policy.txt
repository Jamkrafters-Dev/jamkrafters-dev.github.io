# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: September 8, 2025**

Jamkrafters ("Company," "we," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use our services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information We Collect
We collect the following types of personal information:

**Required Information**
- User ID (userid)
- Device ID (device_id)
- Nickname
- User tag

**Optional Information**
- Bio/Profile description
- Status information
- FCM token (for notification services)
- Notification preferences (fcm_enable)
- Language settings

**Automatically Collected Information**
- IP address
- User agent information
- Device fingerprint
- Service usage logs
- Session data

### 1.2 How We Collect Information
- Direct input during registration and service use
- Through social login integration
- Automatically generated during service usage
- Through cookies and similar technologies

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We collect and use personal information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Service Provision
- User identification and authentication
- Providing chat and messaging services
- Managing friend relationships
- Supporting WebRTC-based P2P communication
- Facilitating invite links and room access

### 2.2 Service Improvement and Operations
- Analyzing service usage statistics
- Developing new features
- Improving service quality
- Maintaining system security

### 2.3 Customer Support
- Responding to customer inquiries
- Providing service-related notifications
- Sending push notifications

### 2.4 Security and Fraud Prevention
- Account security management
- Preventing unauthorized access and spam
- Detecting and preventing service abuse
- Managing bans and restrictions

## 3. Data Retention

### 3.1 General Principle
We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.

### 3.2 Specific Retention Periods
- **User accounts**: Until account deletion
- **Session data**: Until session expiration
- **Refresh tokens**: Until token expiration
- **Invite links**: Until expiration date or completion of use
- **TURN credentials**: Until configured TTL expires
- **Chat room data**: Automatically deleted upon successful P2P connection
- **Signaling messages**: Temporarily stored and deleted after P2P establishment
- **ICE candidates**: Temporarily stored and deleted after P2P connection
- **Log data**: 3 months

### 3.3 Legal Requirements
Information may be retained longer when required by applicable laws and regulations.

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 General Principle
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent.

### 4.2 Exceptions
We may disclose your information in the following circumstances:
- When required by law or legal process
- To comply with government investigations
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- To prevent fraud or security threats
- With your explicit consent
